Take note of:

  • Signing letter "j": make the sign for "i", then rotate your wrist, so that the upward extended pinky finger draws a curve in the air and ends with the pinky pointing in the opposite direction and the palm towards you.

  • Signing letter "z": start by pointing your index finger out, then tracing the written letter "z" in the air by doing a zigzag motion.

  • Signing double letters ("green", "happy"): make the first sign, then shift it to the side to indicate the second occurence of the letter.

  • Double "z" sign: rather than trace it in the air twice, use two fingers (the index and the middle finger) to trace "z" in the air.
  • The sign for "k" and "p" are the same, only "k" is upturned and "p" is downturned. The same applies to the signs for "g" and "q".
